mulching farming

Mulching is the process of covering the topsoil with plant material such as leaves, grass, twigs, crop residues, straw etc. A mulch cover enhances the activity of soil organisms such as earthworms. The residents of the village of Phey near Leh in Ladakh are cultivating a crop that is usually not grown in the fragile, water-scarce cold desert region. Using a technique known as mulching, the village in Leh district has emerged as the hub of organic watermelon cultivation.

Organic/Bio Products

Bioproducts are products that are made from biomass. The meaning of biomass is any type of organic material that is available on a renewable basis. It includes such things as crops and trees, wood and wood wastes, aquatic plants and grasses.
